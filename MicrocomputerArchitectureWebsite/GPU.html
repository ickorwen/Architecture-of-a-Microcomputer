What is a GPU?

A GPU is designed to render graphics, process images, and assist in tasks like gaming and video editing.

Types of GPUs

  • Integrated GPU: Built into the CPU, suitable for general tasks.
  • Discrete GPU: Separate card with dedicated memory, better for intensive graphics tasks.

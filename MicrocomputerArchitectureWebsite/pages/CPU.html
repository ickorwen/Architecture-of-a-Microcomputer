CPU: The Brain of Microcomputers

Overview

The CPU executes instructions from computer programs, making it the essential processing unit.

Structure

  • Control Unit (CU): Directs operations.
  • Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU): Performs calculations and logical operations.
  • Registers: Small, fast storage locations within the CPU.

History and Evolution

From Intel 4004 to modern multi-core processors, CPUs have evolved dramatically.
